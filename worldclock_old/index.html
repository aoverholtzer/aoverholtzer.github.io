World Clock

Check the time, anytime.

Need to know if your colleagues in London have gone home for the night? With the World Clock widget, you don’t need to open an app or do math in your head — just pull down Notification Center.

Add the time zones you want to track. Select the analog or digital clocks. Then check the World Clock widget when you need to know the time.
