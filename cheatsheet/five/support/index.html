iCloud Sync

Why can’t I enabled iCloud Sync?
You need to unlock Cheatsheet Pro on both your iPhone and Watch apps before you can enable iCloud Sync. If you have already purchased Cheatsheet Pro, unlock it for free by tapping “Restore Purchase” on the Cheatsheet Pro page.
Sync Troubleshooting

If your Watch and iPhone are failing to stay in sync, try restarting both devices.

If that doesn’t help, give this a try:

  1. In the Settings app on your iPhone, make sure you are signed in to iCloud. On your Apple Watch, open Settings and confirm that it shows the same name and Apple ID at the top. If your Watch doesn't show the correct Apple ID, you need to sign out of iCloud on your iPhone and then sign back in.
  2. In Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud, make sure iCloud Drive is turned on.
  3. In Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud, make sure Cheatsheet appears in the list of apps and is turned on.
  4. On each of your devices, open the Cheatsheet app and disable iCloud Sync. After disabling iCloud Sync everywhere, please wait a few minutes before continuing.
  5. One-by-one, re-enable iCloud Sync on each of your devices. Let each device finish syncing before re-enabling the next one

Language

How do I set the app to English?

On iOS: Open your Settings app and then select Cheatsheet from the list of apps. Select Language and then choose English.

On Mac: Open System Settings > General > Language & Region. Scroll down to the section labeled Applications and then click the plus button to add an app. Choose Cheatsheet from the list and set its language to English.

How do I set the Watch app to English?

Please check your language settings on your iPhone by going to Settings app > General > Language & Region. If you see unwanted langauges in your “Preferred Languages” list, tap Edit and remove it (or move it below English in the list). This Apple support page may help: https://support.apple.com/HT204031.

If that didn’t help, or you didn’t see unwanted langauges on the list, please check the language setting on your Watch: Open the Apple Watch companion app on the paired iPhone. Go to My Watch > General > Language & Region. Try setting it to “Mirror my Phone” or if there’s a custom list, remove any unwanted languages.

Mac App

Is the Mac app sold-separately?
Yes, Cheatsheet for Mac is a separate purchase from the Cheatsheet Pro subscription for iOS and watchOS. The purchase includes all the Pro features from iOS, such as iCloud sync. Selling the Mac app separately lets me keep the price low for the iOS users while also earning enough from the Mac version to maintain its ongoing development.
