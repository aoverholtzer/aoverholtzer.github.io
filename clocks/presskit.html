Description

Check the time, anytime! Vision Pro has no native clock app, so I’ve come to save the day! Create as many floating “widget” windows as you need and place them all around your spatial computing environment.

  • Beautiful analog and digital clock displays, with options to display seconds and 24-hour time.
  • Track the time around the globe with a world clock widget.
  • Simple UI for adding or editing cities and time zones. Search or browse 10,000+ cities by name.
  • Daylight hours shown visually, based on actual sunrise and sunset times.
  • Totally free!

Clocks is a spinoff of my popular World Clock Widgets for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. It’s the #1 world clock app in App Store, with a 4.7-star rating and over 1 million downloads. So if you like Clocks, please check out World Clock Widgets on your other devices.
