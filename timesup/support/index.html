Using Timers

How do I create a timer?
Tap the button to create a new timer. Give it a name, choose a style, set the duration, and tap Save.
How do I start, pause, or reset a timer?
Tap a timer to open it and start the countdown. Tap the screen again to pause. To reset, tap the reset button or swipe the timer away.
How do I rename a timer?
Open the timer editor by long-pressing a timer and selecting Edit (or tapping the edit button). Tap the name field to change it.
How do I set a custom alert sound?
In the timer editor, tap Alert Sound to choose from the available sounds for when the timer finishes.

Timer Styles

What timer styles are available?
Time’s Up! includes 5 styles:
  • Reveal — a photo is gradually uncovered as time runs out.
  • Pixelate — a photo becomes clearer and sharper as the timer approaches zero.
  • Puzzle — a photo is broken into jigsaw pieces that fall into place as time progresses.
  • Two classic clock face styles for a more traditional look.
How do I choose a style?
In the timer editor, tap Style to select from the available options.
How do I use a photo with my timer?
Choose Reveal, Pixelate, or Puzzle, then tap the image area in the timer editor to pick a photo from your Camera Roll.
Can I change the color of a timer?
Yes. In the timer editor, tap Color to choose a custom color for the timer's background or accent.

Kids & Guided Access

How do I set up a kid-friendly timer?
Create a timer with a fun photo your child loves using the Reveal, Pixelate, or Puzzle style. Then enable Guided Access before handing over your phone so they can't change or stop the timer.
What is Guided Access?
Guided Access is an Apple feature that locks your iPhone or iPad to a single app. While active, your child cannot switch to another app, press the Home button, or change the timer. To enable it:
  1. Go to Settings → Accessibility → Guided Access and turn it on.
  2. Set a passcode in the Guided Access settings.
  3. Open Time’s Up! and triple-click the side button (or Home button) to start a Guided Access session.
How do I stop a Guided Access session?
Triple-click the side button (or Home button) and enter your Guided Access passcode. Then tap End in the top-left corner.

iCloud Sync

How do I sync timers to another device?
Sync is automatic — just make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID on all your devices and that iCloud is enabled for Time’s Up!.
My timers aren't syncing. What should I do?
Check that iCloud is enabled for the app: go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud and make sure Time’s Up! is toggled on. Also check that you have available iCloud storage.

Apple TV & AirPlay

How do I show a timer on my TV?
While a timer is running, tap the AirPlay button and select your Apple TV. The timer will appear full-screen on your TV.
Is there a native Apple TV app?
Yes. Time’s Up! is available on the tvOS App Store so you can use it directly on Apple TV without AirPlay.

Mac How-To

How do I use Time’s Up! on my Mac?
Download Time’s Up! from the Mac App Store. Your saved timers will sync from iPhone and iPad automatically via iCloud.
How do I add a timer:
Click the button, or select File → New Timer.
How do I keep the timer window on top?
Select View → Float Window to keep the timer visible above all other apps.
How do I delete a timer:
Control-click it and select Delete from the menu, or select File → Delete.

Pricing & Purchases

What is free vs. the one-time purchase?
Time’s Up! is free to download. The free version lets you try the app with a limited number of saved timers. The one-time in-app purchase unlocks unlimited timers permanently — no subscription required.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Tap the ••• menu and select Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
Is there educational or VPP pricing?
Yes. Time’s Up! VPP is available on the App Store for schools and education customers purchasing via Apple School Manager.
